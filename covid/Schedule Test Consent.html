Consent Form - Tartan Test

I authorize Carnegie Mellon University's (CMU's) Tartan Testing Laboratory (the
"Laboratory") to conduct collection and testing for SARS-CoV-2 RNA (COVID-19)
through the collection of a saliva specimen from me. I understand that my submission
of a specimen for testing is entirely voluntary, but if I choose to participate
in on-campus or CMU-related activities, including in-person instruction/teaching,
academic study, staff duties, vendor/contractor duties (e.g., janitorial and
foodservice, professional services), research and campus life, etc., it is expected
that I undergo testing for COVID-19.
I acknowledge that the physician who ordered a COVID-19 test pursuant to this
Consent Form is NOT my treating physician or primary care provider and the act of
ordering this test as part of the University's COVID-19 testing program does not
establish a physician-patient relationship between the ordering physician and
myself. If I receive from the Laboratory a suggestion that I may wish to consider
additional medical attention or treatment, or if I determine that I wish to seek
additional medical attention or treatment related to a potential case of COVID-19
or otherwise,
I acknowledge that I must follow up with my regular treating health care provider to
discuss the next steps in my care. I assume complete and full responsibility to
take appropriate action with regard to my test results. I agree to seek medical
advice, care and treatment from my primary health care provider or other
healthcare professionals if I have questions or concerns, or if my condition
worsens. The ordering physician is not responsible or liable for any course of
treatment, diagnosis, or any other health care service I may elect to receive as a
result of receiving this test or otherwise.
