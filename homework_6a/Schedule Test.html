Instructions - Tartan Test

 

 

If you have any symptoms or are in isolation/quarantine or have tested positive
for COVID19 in the past 90 days, do not participate in Tartan Testing.
Stay home if you are sick and call UHS at 412-268-2157, option 2.
Tartan testing by appointment only and is provided at no cost.
Appointments are ONLY available in one week increments.
